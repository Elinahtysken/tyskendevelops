Hi, I'm Elina Maria.

I started my career as a journalist, writing for NSD, EFN, and other publications. From there I moved into marketing, working with brands like Matsmart and Bilbolaget, and I'm currently at Scream. Over time, I've found myself drawn more and more into coding — building things from scratch, solving real problems, and shipping ideas fast. Now I combine all of it: storytelling, strategy, and technology.
